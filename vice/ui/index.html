Uninstall Vice

Run this command in a terminal to remove Vice from your system. The daemon, desktop entry, and settings will be deleted. Your clips in ~/Videos/Vice are not touched.

vice uninstall

Welcome to Vice

Your Medal.tv-style game clip recorder for Linux. Here's the quick rundown:

⌨️
Press F9 to save a clip Saves the last 15 seconds of gameplay. Change the key in Settings.
⏺
Double-tap F9 to start a session recording Records continuously until you double-tap F9 again. Single-tap F9 during a session marks a highlight at that timestamp.
🎬
Click any thumbnail to watch Opens the full video viewer with prev/next navigation and seek bar.
🔗
Share button = shareable link Works outside your WiFi — embeds in Discord automatically.
📍
Press H in the viewer to add highlights Mark important moments, colour-code them, and rename them freely.
